REG Open Source Service Area support the Turing in engaging with the wider open source community through a variety of conferences and community groups.

## FOSDEM

[FOSDEM](https://fosdem.org/) is a free open source conference held annually in Brussels. For the past few years, the Turing have been actively attending and contributing to the FOSDEM programme.

- **FOSDEM 2024** — We attended and supported the Open Science devroom, Python devroom and HPC, Big Data & Data Science devroom and the Linux on Mobile stand. We wrote a [blog post](https://medium.com/@turinghut23/the-turing-and-open-source-at-fosdem24-d19bb46e48e8) and gave a REG tech talk about our experiences.
- **FOSDEM 2025** — Again, we attended and had presence across Open Science, HPC, Big Data & Data Science, Python, and FOSS on Mobile devrooms. We wrote a [blog post](https://medium.com/@turinghut23/highlights-and-reflections-from-fosdem-2025-987fabd26932) on the REG blog about our experiences.
- **FOSDEM 2026** — Again, we attended and had presence across the Open Research, HPC, Big Data & Data Science, Python and FOSS on Mobile devrooms. Our blog post is a WIP.

## CURIOSS

[CURIOSS](https://curioss.org/) is a network of Open Source Programme Offices at research and academic institutions. We've recently joined CURIOSS and are looking forward to engaging with their network and resources.
